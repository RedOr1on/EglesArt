Egle's Art

About Artist


Egle works mostly in acrylics, coloured pencils and mixed media with strong focus on colours. Many of her paintings reflect nature-based themes, such as landscapes, animals and flora; she also paints portraits to capture moods and expressions. Her main sources of inspirations are nature, travelling and a mixture of hard rock, metal and acoustic music. In terms of style, Egle’s artistic expression is somewhat influenced by Impressionistic paintings and their use of colour. She also has a technical background in mechanical engineering and loves to experiment with different techniques and materials while maintaining her artistic style. You can find more of Egle's art including work-in-progress captures on Instagram @RedOr1on and on Dojo Art Store Facebook page.
